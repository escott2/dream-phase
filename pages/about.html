About

What is Dream Phase?

Dream Phase is a tool that helps you organize your goals and dreams for each phase of life. It allows you to create a list of goals and dreams for each phase, and track your progress as you work towards achieving them.

How do I get started?

  • To begin using Dream Phase, create a new phase by entering a name and clicking the ‘Add Phase’ button.
  • Navigate to the phase page to add goals and dreams by entering them and selecting ‘Add Dream.’
  • Mark goals and dreams as completed to track your progress through each phase of life.
